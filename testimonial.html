Greetings! we are A&A service, and here is the content in which we will express our journey throughout the task that we made. So, to begin with, we brainstormed what is the most prevalent problem here in the Philippines that we cannot tackle, and we settled on sex education because most of the teenagers here in the Philippines are experiencing adolescent pregnancy and lack understanding about sex education

So to begin with, we decided to start by brainstorming what the most pervasive issue in the country is that we are struggling to address, so we settled on sexual education since most teenagers in the Philippines are experiencing adolescent pregnancies and fail to understand the importance of sex education. As the proposal begins, we decided that raising awareness and advocating for change is the best way to adress the issue.

The purpose of this website is to provide the necessary information about us as a service and the knowledge we collected with the objective to increase awareness of the importance of SexEd.
