About our brand :

Our brand is designed for people who are lacking in knowledge about safe intercourse or sex education because nowadays, we witness teenager like us that experience teenage pregnancy. Our objective for our service is to raise awareness and advocate the safe and right procedures on to people - not only for those who fell victim but also to the rest of the masses.

Hence, comes the name “Advocate & Awareness”


Goals/Objective :

As we have stated our goal is advocating and educating the people in order for them to have the right knowledge about safe intercourse, as well as raising awareness on the dangers and wrong practices that could lead to unfortunate outcomes. Avoiding those types of occurrences to happen, securing their future.

Service :

We have made a YouTube channel to teach and provide understanding/comprehension about sex education. Aimed primarily to teenagers,
The content we will produce will help raise awareness and advocate on how to properly handle the concept of sex education.
